S O L A S.  I N T E R P L A N E T A R Y.  T R A N S I T.  S Y S T E M.











The Solas Interplanetary Transit System is our biggest project. It is a station made from the combined efforts of several different companies. This station will be where all of the Revelation:7 rockets dock after launching off earth. Once all of the rockets are docked, this station will be the main hub that transports Revelation:7 units to different planets for colonization. The first destination for SITS is Mars. Two Revelation:7 units will be dispatched onto the planet with all of the supplies neccesary to create a self-sustaining colony. Using solar technology, we hope to provide colonies with power and conversion plants to convert oxygen and hydrogen into usable water. Oxygen from water can also be achieved with solar power and electricity. Mars is only the first step however, after Mars is sufficiently colonized, we plan to use it as a supply station. Expanding it into cities and greenhouses with crops. Essentially providing fresh food after the journey from Earth and on from Mars.
